## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Quick Add from Menu
The **Menu** buttons have pre-filled foods and exercises tailored to your fitness goal. This is the fastest way to log!

### Understand Your Stats
- **Net Calories** = Food - Exercise
- **Remaining Calories** = Target - Net Calories
- Your target is automatically calculated for your goal

### Track Consistently
The GitHub-style graph shows your consistency. Try to log something every day!

### Use Realistic Calorie Estimates
- For food: Check labels or estimate (google: "calories in [food]")
- For exercise: Use fitness watches or online calculators

### Weight Logging
- Log once per day at the same time (morning recommended)
- Don't obsess over daily changes - look at weekly trends
- Weight fluctuates due to water, food volume, etc.
