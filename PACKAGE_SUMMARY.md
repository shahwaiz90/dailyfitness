## â“ FAQ

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! Data stays on your device in localStorage. No tracking or cloud servers.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: Yes! After first load, it works offline. Charts need libraries loaded once though.

**Q: Can I sync across devices?**
A: Not automatically, but you can manually export/import data.

**Q: What if I clear my browser cache?**
A: You'll lose your data. Use "Reset Profile" button instead to clear intentionally.

**Q: Can I customize the foods/exercises?**
A: Yes! Use custom entry instead of menu. Just type name and calories.

**Q: How often should I log weight?**
A: Daily is ideal (morning), but every other day is fine. Weekly minimum.

**Q: Can I use on mobile?**

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
